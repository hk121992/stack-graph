# Authoring handbook pages

**Status: scaffold — to be authored.** This page will codify the page conventions
(mirroring the Be Civic handbook): section folders `NN-section/` with a `README.md`
index + `NN-page.md` leaves, prefix-stripped slugs, frontmatter (`title`, `type`,
`read-when`, `related`, terse `status`), an h2-only table of contents, and the
`index.json` page-graph.
